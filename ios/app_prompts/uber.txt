This file describes what typically appears when using Uber on iOS.

Booking a ride:
When you open Uber, you usually see a map or home screen with a prominent destination entry control often labeled like "Where to?". After selecting the destination entry, a planning screen appears with pickup information and a destination input field. When you type a destination, a list of matching places appears with name, address, and distance. After selecting a destination, ride options are shown in a bottom sheet with ride types such as UberX, Comfort, and Black with ETA and price. A payment row and a primary action button to confirm appear. After confirming, a status screen shows "Finding your ride" and then driver details once matched.

Scheduling a ride for later:
On the home screen, a scheduling control often labeled "Later" or a time selector appears near the destination entry. Selecting it shows date and time pickers. After selecting a time and destination, ride options appear as usual with a Schedule button instead of Request.

Cancelling a ride:
After a ride is requested or a driver is matched, the trip screen appears. Selecting the trip details or a menu icon shows a Cancel Ride option. A confirmation prompt may appear with a potential cancellation fee.

Checking ride history:
After selecting the menu or profile icon, an option for Your Trips or Activity appears. Selecting it shows a list of past rides with dates, destinations, and prices.

Rating a driver:
After a trip is completed, a rating screen appears. Stars for rating and an optional comment field are displayed. A Submit button finalizes the rating.

Common situations that may appear:
- Destination ambiguity with multiple results
- Pickup location mismatch
- No drivers available
- Payment method prompts or errors
- Surge pricing notifications

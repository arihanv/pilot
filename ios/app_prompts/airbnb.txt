This file describes what typically appears when using Airbnb on iOS.

Booking a stay:
When you open Airbnb, you usually see a home screen with a search bar near the top. Below that are destination suggestions, categories, and featured listings. After selecting the search bar, you typically see fields to enter a location, dates, and number of guests. Once entered, a list of matching properties appears with image, price per night, rating, and location. When you select a property, the listing page shows photos, host information, amenities, and house rules. After reviewing, a button to check availability or reserve is displayed. The booking screen shows dates, guests, price breakdown, cleaning fees, and total cost. A payment method and confirmation button are displayed.

Messaging a host:
On a listing page, a Contact Host button may appear. Selecting it opens a message compose screen. After typing a message and sending, the conversation appears in your inbox.

Checking booking details:
After selecting the Trips tab, upcoming and past reservations are listed. Selecting a reservation shows details including address, check-in instructions, and host contact.

Cancelling a reservation:
After selecting a reservation from the Trips tab, a cancellation option appears. Selecting it shows the cancellation policy and any refund details. A confirmation button finalizes the cancellation.

Searching for a listing:
The search bar accepts location input. After entering dates and guests, results appear. Filter options for price range, property type, amenities, and rating help refine results.

Common situations that may appear:
- Multiple listings with similar prices
- Date availability selection
- Guest count adjustments
- Cleaning or service fees added at checkout
- Payment method prompts

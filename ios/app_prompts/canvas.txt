This file describes what typically appears when using Canvas on iOS.

Viewing and submitting assignments:
When you open Canvas, you usually see a dashboard with a list of your courses. Each course shows the course name and may display recent announcements or upcoming assignments. After selecting a course, the course page appears with navigation options such as Announcements, Assignments, Discussions, Grades, Modules, and People. After selecting Assignments, a list of assignments appears with title, due date, and submission status. After selecting an assignment, the detail page shows instructions, due date, and a submission area. Options to submit text, upload a file, or enter a URL may appear.

Replying to a discussion:
After selecting Discussions in a course, a list of discussion topics appears with title and reply count. After selecting a discussion, the thread appears with the prompt and previous replies. A reply input area appears at the bottom. When you type a reply and submit, the response appears in the discussion thread.

Checking grades:
After selecting Grades in a course, a list of assignments with scores and feedback appears. Selecting an assignment shows detailed grade information and any instructor comments.

Viewing announcements:
After selecting Announcements in a course, a list of announcements appears sorted by date. Selecting one shows the full announcement text.

Searching for a course or content:
A search bar may appear on the dashboard. When you type a query, matching courses and content are displayed.

Downloading course materials:
In Modules or Files within a course, documents and files are listed. Selecting a file opens or downloads it for viewing.

Common situations that may appear:
- Multiple courses listed
- Assignment submission type requirements
- File upload prompts
- Due date warnings
- Grade display alongside assignments

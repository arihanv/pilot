This file describes what typically appears when using Lyft on iOS.

Booking a ride:
When you open Lyft, you usually see a map or home screen with a destination entry area displayed near the top. You may also see recent destinations or ride suggestions. After selecting the destination entry area, you typically see a planning screen with your pickup location and a field to enter your destination. When you type a destination, a list of matching places appears with name and address. After selecting a destination, ride options are shown in a panel with ride types such as Standard and XL with price estimate and arrival time. When a ride type is selected, a confirmation button appears to request the ride. When requested, a status screen shows "Finding a driver" and then driver details once matched.

Scheduling a ride:
On the planning screen, a scheduling option may appear to set a pickup time for later. After selecting a date and time, ride options appear with a Schedule button.

Cancelling a ride:
After a ride is requested, the trip screen shows a menu or options button. Selecting it shows a Cancel Ride option. A confirmation prompt may appear with a cancellation fee warning.

Checking ride history:
After selecting the menu or profile icon, a Ride History option appears. Selecting it shows a list of past rides with dates, destinations, and prices.

Adding a stop:
On the planning screen or during an active ride, an option to add a stop may appear. Selecting it opens a destination input for the additional stop.

Common situations that may appear:
- Multiple destinations with similar names
- Pickup location requiring adjustment
- No drivers available
- Payment method prompts
- Wait and Save options
